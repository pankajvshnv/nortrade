About Us

NORTRADE is a Swiss architectural visualization studio dedicated to helping developers, architects, interior designers and real estate professionals present projects with clarity, emotion and impact. Through photorealistic renderings, cinematic animations and virtual staging, we transform concepts into compelling visual experiences that support marketing, sales and investment decisions.

Interior & Exterior Renderings

Photorealistic Renderings

High-end interior and exterior visualizations that bring unbuilt architecture to life with exceptional realism, lighting accuracy, and emotional impact.

Home Staging & 360° Tours

Virtual Experiences

Immersive 360° walkthroughs and digital home staging that allow buyers to explore and connect with spaces interactively before construction begins.

3D Floor Plans & Masterplans

Spatial Planning

Detailed 3D floor plans and large-scale masterplans that clearly communicate layouts, circulation, and urban integration to investors and buyers.

Animations & Aerials

Dynamic Perspectives

Cinematic animations and breathtaking aerial drone renderings that showcase your development's scale, context, and atmosphere in motion.

Modelling & CGI

CGI Marketing

Precise architectural modeling and bespoke CGI assets tailored to elevate your real estate marketing campaigns across all digital and print platforms.
